The Boxmodel Laboratory

Our lab is just a playground for testing components, code and graphics. We decided we would place some of these items live now and again, so feel free to experiment with our lab items below.

Please note that as this is the lab section, some of these exploratory items haven't been tested in all browsers or on all devices. Sometimes our creatives and developers simply unleash themselves into the realm of discovery and possibility, probing for solutions, messing about....or just simply upgrading their status in the digital geek world.

For our final client based projects, there's nothing cleaner or more functional than our code...but as was drummed into us at school, it's sometimes useful to show our workings out and rough drafts.

For the record, if you use a remotely up to date browser such as Chrome, everything should work fine.

Enjoy!
